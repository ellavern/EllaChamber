What they're doing: flooding the feed with fresh video. 3 of their top 5 ads launched July 2nd and are already hitting top impression rank after just 7 days, paired with long running image statics (33 to 38 days) as always on support. Gap for us: our video cadence is too slow. Gymshark is launching multiple videos at once to find winners fast.
Oner Active Top 5 by impression rank · 26 to 110 days active
Video, 110 days
Video, 26 days
Carousel, 8 slides
What they're doing: almost entirely video. 4 of their top 5 ads are video, with a heavy 8 slide carousel as their secondary format. Standout signal: their top video has run for 110 days straight, proving a strong video concept doesn't need constant refreshing. Gap for us: we're not testing big carousels and we don't have a single video built to run long term.
Takeaway for BuffBunny: both competitors point to the same fix from different angles. Gymshark's rapid fire video launches (3 fresh cuts in one week) show the value of testing several video concepts at once instead of one at a time, and Oner Active's 110 day video plus 8 slide carousel show that a strong video or carousel concept can be evergreen rather than something to rotate constantly. Speed up video testing cadence, and build a bigger, bundle style carousel to chase both formats at once.
