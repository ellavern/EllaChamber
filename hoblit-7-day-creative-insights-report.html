Why It Matters, Key Learnings
01
Video is winning, and it's not close.
Video ads are earning clicks at almost double the rate of static images this week. That's why every new initiative above leans on video or interactive formats first.
02
Small budget tests reveal where to go bigger.
The Ford result proved itself on very little spend. We treat signals like this as an early warning system, telling us where to invest more before committing real budget.
03
Aging creative quietly costs you.
Older evergreen assets are still running but slowing down week over week. The new dealership, family, and interior content exists specifically to get ahead of that decline instead of reacting to it.
